About Me

Hello! I am a Research Associate working in the Speech Group of the Machine Intelligence Laboratory, Engineering Department of the University of Cambridge (CUED).

In 2021, I obtained my PhD degree at the University of Birmingham, supervised by Prof. Martin Russell and Dr. Peter Jančovič. My PhD project is about computer analysis for children's non-native English speech.

From April 2021, I started my Research Associate position at the Cambridge University. I am involved in the Automated Language Teaching and Assessment (ALTA) project, funded by Cambridge Assessment, and the Multimodal Video Search by Example (MVSE) project funded by EPSRC.

Research Interests

  • Automatic Speech Recognition
  • Speaker Recognition and Diarisation
  • Mispronunciation Detection
  • Information Retrieval
  • Natural Language Processing
