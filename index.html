Mengjie Qian

Hello! I am a Research Associate working in the Speech Group of the Machine Intelligence Laboratory, Engineering Department of the University of Cambridge (CUED). I obtained my PhD degree at the University of Birmingham, supervised by Prof. Martin Russell, in 2021. My PhD project is about computer analysis for children's non-native English speech. From April 2021, I started working on the Automated Language Teaching and Assessment (ALTA) project, funded by Cambridge Assessment. My research interests include speech recognition, speaker recognition diarisation, natural language processing.
