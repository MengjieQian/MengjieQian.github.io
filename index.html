About Me

Hello! I am a Research Associate in the Speech Group of the Machine Intelligence Laboratory, Engineering Department of the University of Cambridge (CUED).

My Research Associate position at the Cambridge University was started from April 2021, and I work with Professor Mark Gales and Dr. Kate Knill. I am involved in the Automated Language Teaching and Assessment (ALTA) project, funded by Cambridge Assessment, and the Multimodal Video Search by Example (MVSE) project funded by EPSRC.

In 2021, I obtained my PhD degree at the University of Birmingham, supervised by Prof. Martin Russell and Dr. Peter Jančovič. My PhD project is about computer analysis for children's non-native English speech.

Research Interests

  • Automatic Speech Recognition
  • Language Modelling
  • Speaker Recognition and Diarisation
  • Mispronunciation Detection
  • Information Retrieval
  • Natural Language Processing
