Mengjie Qian 钱梦洁

Research Associate, University of Cambridge

I am a Research Associate in the Machine Intelligence Laboratory at the University of Cambridge, working with Prof. Kate Knill and Prof. Mark Gales.

My research focuses on speech foundation models (SFMs) and large language models (LLMs), particularly their application in education and healthcare for low-resource languages.
